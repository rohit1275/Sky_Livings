Our Story

Welcome to Sky Livings – your home away from home near Lovely Professional University, located in the heart of Law Gate.

Established in the year 2020, Sky Livings has been committed to providing comfortable, safe, and affordable accommodation for students and working professionals. With over 25 well-maintained apartments, we offer modern living spaces equipped with all essential amenities to make your stay as smooth and homely as possible.

At Sky Livings, we don't just provide rooms — we provide a lifestyle that supports growth, learning, and peace of mind. Whether you're a student beginning your academic journey or a professional looking for a hassle-free stay, Sky Livings is here for you.
