Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Payment

How can I book a room at Sky Livings?

You can book a room through our website by filling out the inquiry form, calling us directly, or visiting our office. We'll guide you through the booking process and help you choose the perfect accommodation.

What is the payment process?

We accept multiple payment methods including bank transfer, UPI, and cash. A security deposit is required at the time of booking, and monthly rent is payable in advance. We provide detailed payment receipts for all transactions.

Facilities & Amenities

What facilities are included in the rent?

Our rent includes high-speed WiFi, 24/7 electricity backup, regular housekeeping, security services, maintenance, and access to common areas. Additional services like laundry and food can be arranged at extra cost.

Is food provided in the PG?

We offer optional food services with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. You can choose from monthly meal plans or pay-as-you-go options. Our food is prepared in hygienic conditions and served fresh.

Rules & Regulations

What are the visiting hours?

Visitors are allowed between 9 AM to 9 PM. All visitors must register at the reception and provide valid ID proof. For security reasons, overnight stays are not permitted.

What are the check-in/check-out timings?

Check-in time is 12 PM and check-out time is 11 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged with prior notice, subject to availability and additional charges.

Location & Transportation

How far is the PG from LPU?

Our properties are located within 1-2 km from LPU, making it convenient for students. We provide shuttle services during peak hours, and public transport is easily accessible.

Is parking available?

Yes, we provide secure parking spaces for two-wheelers and bicycles. Parking is available on a first-come-first-serve basis and requires prior registration.
